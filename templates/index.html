Questions

Before you ask

Is my resume data stored anywhere?

Your resume is stored only to power your dashboard history. You can delete your account and all data from your profile settings at any time.

What file formats can I upload?

PDF and DOCX, up to 5MB. We parse contact info, education, skills, projects, experience and certifications from either format.

How is the ATS score calculated?

Across contact information, education, experience, projects, skills, formatting, keyword match and length — each weighted and combined into a score out of 100.

Do I need to pay for this?

No. This runs entirely on free-tier tools: Flask, SQLite and the Groq API.
